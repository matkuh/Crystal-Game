The Mad King Needs Your Help!

Instructions

- The Mad King recently burned his pyromancer alive and you are to take his place! He wants you to create as much Wildfire as you can, the only problem is the recipie was on the pyromancer when he was set ablaze!

- You find 4 ingredients on the table in the pyromancers lab. Each ingredient has a randomized potency and it is your job to combine them together to reach the required potency to make the wildfire.

- If you can get your current potency to match the required potency, you will make the wildfire, and if not, you will die!

- Every time you succeed or fail to make the wildfire, the potency of each ingredient will change again.
